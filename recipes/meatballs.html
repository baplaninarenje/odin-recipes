Meatballs

Meet Porcupine Meatballs: The Retro Dinner That Deserves a Comeback

“These are better than the ones mom used to make (sorry, mom)!”

Ingredients

  • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Shape meatballs as directed above and place them in an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  • Mix tomato sauce, stock, Worcestershire sauce, and sugar in a bowl; pour over meatballs.
  • Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for 15 more minutes.

Steps

  1. Make the meatballs.
  2. Heat oil and brown the meatballs in a hot skillet.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients to the skillet.
  4. Continue cooking until the meatballs are cooked through.
